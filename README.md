# Instahyre-jobanalytics

During mid-May 2023, an analysis was conducted on the job postings and employment opportunities available on Instahyre.com, a professional opportunity and job listings company. To gather the data, a Python script utilizing the Selenium and BeautifulSoup4 libraries was used to scrape the dynamic website. Over 650 job postings and information on more than 200 companies were collected and organized into a pandas DataFrame.

The data was cleaned and explored using numpy and pandas, and three main tables were created: Jobs, Companies, and Job Details. Primary keys were generated for Jobs and Companies, and various data points such as job ID, designation, company name, company size, HR contact, required skills, and year of founding were included.

Once the tables were established, further analysis was performed using pandas to gain insights from the data. The tables were linked based on the generated primary keys, and the data was exported into comma-separated value files. These files were then used for additional analysis and data visualization in Microsoft Excel.

The analysis focused on aggregating and interpreting the data to understand the job market. Key metrics such as the number of job openings in different cities, industry distribution, company sizes, locations, and experience levels were measured. The results were presented in an Excel dashboard and a presentation was created to highlight significant visualizations, metrics, and insights.

The findings indicated a strong concentration of job openings in Bengaluru, which had the highest number of opportunities overall. The Information & Technology (IT) industry emerged as the dominant employer, with over 50% of the jobs being related to software/technology or data analytics. These insights suggest that the IT industry has a substantial impact on the job market, offering numerous employment opportunities to younger, less experienced job seekers.

The analysis of job postings and employment opportunities on Instahyre.com revealed valuable insights about the job market during mid-May 2023. The data emphasized the dominance of Bengaluru as a job hub and highlighted the significant presence of the IT industry, particularly in software/technology and data analytics roles.
